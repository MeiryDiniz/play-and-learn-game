Instructions

Spelling Game is an enjoyable game for kids learn and have fun.

Click on: Play to be redirected to the game page.

When the game page is loaded an image will be displayed.

So, you can click on the indicated space and type your answer.

If you think the word is a bit trick, you can click on: Next Image

without your score be affected.

After type your answer you can click on: Check Answer or Enter

on your keyboard to have it checked.

If the answer is correct, console will log: Well done! You got it! and

your Correct Answers will be updated.

if not, console will log a message with your answer and the correct one, so your

Wrong Answers score will be updated,

If you ask to check the answer without type anything console will log: You have to insert an answer!,

and a new image will be loaded. It won't affect your score.

To go back to the home page you click can on Home or on the name of the game any time.
